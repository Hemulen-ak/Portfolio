About Me

"I've always been drawn to the space where logic meets creativity."

My journey started in a call center. Listening to people, solving problems, and learning patience. It then moved to classrooms, where I taught Computer Science and English, helping others find their voice.

Eventually, I found my own new voice in code and AI. I realized that technology doesn't have to be cold; it can be a tool for connection and expression. Now, I spend my days exploring large language models, crafting prompts, and building digital tools that feel natural and useful.

I chose this path not for the hype, but for the quiet satisfaction of making things work.
